About Us

Pune Institute of Computer Technology has been participating int he NSS scheme since 2010.The NSS unit consists of a 100 volunteers.The motto of NSS "NOT ME, BUT YOU" upholds the need for selfless service.

Mission

The selfless service of PICT NSS underlines that the welfare of anindividual is ultimately dependent on the welfare of society as a whole.

Vision

Our vision is to build the youth with the mind and spirit to serve the society and work for the social uplift of the down-trodden masses of our nation as a movement
